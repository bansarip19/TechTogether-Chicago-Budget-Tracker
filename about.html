    This website helps users track their finances, manage their budget and find smart ways to spend their money. It takes users on an adventure in Chicago to explore how different spending choices have different savings consequences. It includes a budget tracker tool where users can enter their own income and expenses. Plus: spending tips and info on growing your money with savings accounts!
